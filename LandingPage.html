Welcome to pirates
Pirates is a non-profit student activity that was founded in 2009. Its main mission is to help bridge the gap between students and the market. Pirates’ job is to give students the chance to flourish and prove their worth and find the diamond in the rough. Furthermore, it aims to prepare students for the future.
